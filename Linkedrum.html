    FAQs

    Have questions? We've got answers. Our FAQ section is designed to help you find information quickly and get back to enjoying your renting experience

    Can I use automation or scrapping tools?

    Of course, and that’s the whole point of our service. Scrapping tools that we recommend include Phantombuster, Captain Data, Texau… You can also use your favorite tool. We recommend automation tools: The Growth Machine, HeyReach, and Waalaxy. We also have an offer with Waalaxy. The important thing is to use 1 scrap tool and 1 automation tool for 1 rented LinkedIn account to avoid exceeding LinkedIn quotas. Don’t hesitate to ask us for advice!

    Are our accounts leased with Sales Navigator?

    No, our profiles are rented without LinkedIn Sales Navigator. However, you can add Sales Navigator by subscribing directly to the profile’s Linkedin. We will try to give you a profile with the Sales Navigator trial period to save money if possible

    What profile information can I modify?

    Absolutely everything! Except the information that is related to a real person

    Do the profiles take on someone's identity?

    Yes, of course! We have a reliable base of people. With that approach, we always can reverify accounts in case of some restrictions

    What automation tools can I use?

    You can use all the automation tools you own as long as you do not use several tools on the same account. For example, using Waalaxy and The Growth Machine at the same time is prohibited. Here are the tools that we have already tested without success: Waalaxy, La Growth Machine, Phantombuster, HeyReach, Lusha, Captain Data, Neo Deal, Link Helper, Expandi, DuxSoup… If you have any doubts, do not hesitate to ask us 🙂

    If my account ever gets banned, what happens?

    If this happens, we carry out an audit to see if you have used only one automation tool per LinkedIn account. If this is the case, we will replace it for you in less than 24 hours. Also, we will try to unblock the account with a re-verification process

    If the account is heated, what is the point of having a Sales Navigator account?

    We recommend equipping a single profile with Sales Navigator to facilitate the creation of your databases and achieve precise targeting. There is no need to enable Sales Navigator on all of your accounts, as it does not impact your quotas. This way, you optimize your expenses while benefiting from the advantages of this tool

    How many relationships do the accounts have and what are the types of connections?

    Our accounts for rent have more than 500 connections, obtained manually on all types of profiles (CEO, business developer, etc.). All of these relationships are real! However, they cannot be specialized on your targets, it is your campaigns that will specialize your account
